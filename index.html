Some popular tasks we've been handling

Online Stores

We've edited and proofread many product descriptions and user manuals for international sellers on Amazon, Ebay, Shopify, Etsy and individual websites.

You can simply submit links to the pages you want us to look at.

Blog Posts / News Articles

We work with native and non-native English speaking bloggers and publishers. It's always smart to have someone check your article before you publish it.

For regular customers we offer editing articles directly from their Wordpress system using a user created for our team.

Pre-Launch Websites / Apps

We proofread text in websites and apps for Entrepreneurs from all over the world. Sometimes it's just the About Us page and sometimes entire websites.

For large scale projects, we can do editing directly in the HTML files and even push changes to git, so you can easily see what we fixed.

CVs / Job Applications

We've helped polish many CVs and received great responses from clients.

This is probably the most important time to seek a professional editor. You don't want to miss your dream job because you had a few misspellings in your CV.

Books / E-books

Whether it's a recipes E-book for kindle or the novel you've been writing for years, we will make sure your readers will enjoy a book written in beautiful English.

If you have your book as a Microsoft Word document (.doc/.docx) that's great but even if not, we'll do our best to work with whatever file format you have.

Academic Papers and Essays

We've edited Academic research papers and students essays on various different fields and subjects.

In case the subject of your paper requires some specific domain knowledge, please contact us to make sure we can provide you with editing services in this domain.

It's easier than you might think

1
Submit a new task

We'll ask for your email address and the text you want us to proofread or edit.

You can submit your text in a Microsoft Word file or any other file type, we'll do our best to work with whatever you submit.

You can also submit links to web-pages you want us to edit or to documents stored in cloud storage (e.g. GoogleDrive, Dropbox, OneDrive).

2
Accept our offer

Shortly after submiting, you'll recieve an email from us with the exact price for your task and our estimated time to finish it. Simple tasks usually take 24 hours or less.

The email will contain a link to a payment page, you'll be able to pay using your credit/debit card or PayPal account.

Once you've paid, we will be automatically notified and start working on your task.

3
Recieve the result

We will send the result by email.

Normally we attach two Microsoft Word documents. One with your fixed text that's ready for publishing, the other with the original text and inline comments on every mistake we've fixed.

We might be able to provide the result in a different format, you can request it when you submit your task.
