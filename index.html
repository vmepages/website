It's easier than you might think

1
Submit a new task

We'll ask for your email address and the text you want us to proofread or edit.

You can submit your text in a Microsoft Word file or any other file type, we'll do our best to work with whatever you submit.

You can also submit links to web-pages you want us to edit or to documents stored in cloud storage (e.g. GoogleDrive, Dropbox, OneDrive).

2
Accept our offer

Shortly after submiting, you'll recieve an email from us with the exact price for your task and our estimated time to finish it. Simple tasks usually take 24 hours or less.

The email will contain a link to a payment page, you'll be able to pay using your credit/debit card or PayPal account.

Once you've paid, we will be automatically notified and start working on your task.

3
Recieve the result

We will send the result by email.

Normally we attach two Microsoft Word documents. One with your fixed text that's ready for publishing, the other with the original text and inline comments on every mistake we've fixed.

We might be able to provide the result in a different format, you can request it when you submit your task.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to submit a Microsoft Word document?

No, you can submit any file format.

We'll do our best to work with the text from your file. If we can't process your file we will contact you and ask for a different file format.

Can I submit links to webpages?

Yes, you can link to any webpage and tell us which part you want us to look at.

If you have a page that's not publicly accessible (like a draft post in Wordpress) you can save the HTML file to your computer and send it to us.

How much time until I receive the result?

Simple tasks of up to a few pages are usually done within 24 hours.

Larger tasks may take longer, depending on the size. After you submit your task we'll review it and email you an offer along with the time we expect to finish.

I need something urgently, can I ask for a rush-order?

Yes, you can request it when you submit your task, it usually costs 50% more.

If we have an editor available immediately then we will quickly send you an offer with the best finish time we can.

What will the result look like?

Normally we email back two Microsoft Word documents. One with your fixed text that's ready for publishing, the other with the original text and inline comments on every mistake we've fixed.

We might be able to provide the result in a different format, you can request it when you submit your task.

I need you to edit an entire book, is there a discount?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for large scale tasks.

The discount will be based on the size of your task, you can submit your task regularly and we'll email you the offer including the discount. You can also contact us and generally describe the size of your task and we'll give you an estimated price.
